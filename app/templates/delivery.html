Delivery Information

Shipping Areas

We currently deliver across all major cities and rural areas in Rwanda. Whether you’re in Kigali or a remote farm village, we make sure your pineapples arrive fresh and fast.

Delivery Time

Orders placed before 2 PM are typically processed the same day. You can expect your delivery within 24–48 hours depending on your location.

  • Kigali: Next-day delivery
  • Southern & Western provinces: Within 2 days
  • Remote villages: Within 3 days max

Delivery Cost

We keep delivery fees low so everyone can enjoy healthy food. Standard shipping is:

  • RWF 1000 in Kigali
  • RWF 1500 to other provinces
  • Free delivery for orders over RWF 10,000

Packaging & Health Safety

All items are packed with care in hygienic, eco-friendly materials. Our team ensures zero cross-contamination, especially for products labeled diabetic-friendly — no added sugars, safe for people with pre-diabetic or diabetic conditions.

Need Help?

If you have questions or want to arrange custom delivery, contact us directly at +250787133704 or email support@pineappleshop.rw.
