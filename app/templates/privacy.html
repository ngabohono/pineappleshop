Privacy Policy

At Pineapple Shop Rwanda, we care deeply about your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or purchase from our store.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Info: Name, phone number, email, delivery address
  • Health Preferences: (optional) notes for diabetic-friendly options
  • Payment Info: Secured through trusted third-party services
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (for analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and deliver products safely
  • To recommend healthier or diabetic-safe items (if relevant)
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send order updates and promotional emails (opt-out anytime)

3. Sharing & Security

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share necessary details with delivery services or secure payment processors. All sensitive information is encrypted and stored with care.

4. Your Rights

  • You may request a copy of your data
  • You may request we delete your data at any time
  • You may update your preferences by contacting us

5. Contact Us
